Beef and Prime Rib Rub

Description

This prime rib rub is especially delicious on prime rib that is roasted low and slow but it's great for any cut of beef.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoons Montreal steak seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun spice
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Combine salt, pepper, paprika, steak seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, Cajun spice, thyme, rosemary, and basil in a container with an airtight lid.
